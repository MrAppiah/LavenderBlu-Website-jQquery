About — Us

Lavender Blu is an independent after school club which is owned and managed by qualified early years practitioners with over 15 years childcare experience. We aim to promote a safe, stable, and enjoyable environment for the children who attend. We currently run both a holiday and after-school club. Our aim is to provide a magical environment where children can share, learn, and grow.

Services & What we offer

Activities

Arts and crafts will include free drawing, clay and playdough.

A queit area where children are encouraged to read with staff and begin homework tasks set by the school.

Construction will include various building materials.

Adult-led activities,free drawing chalk and wipe boards.

Outdoor Play. Board games. Video games. Dressing up and imaginative role play.

VIP days will include: Street dance, Multi sports, Judo, Trips, etc

Snacks

Snacks will be set out so that the children can sit and enjoy their snack in the company of their friends.

The staff will always try and promote healthy eating; fresh water will always be available as well as fresh fruits, crackers, toasted sandwich, wraps, waffles carrots, tomatoes, and cucumber.

We will also provide snack for children who arrive later after attending extra curricular club.

Holiday Club

Lavender Blu provides a holiday club for parents/guardians who are in need of extra childcare throughout the half term.

Our holiday club provides a wider range of activities and events for all children with different interests to enjoy.

We're constantly introducing new activities to challenge and excite our children to enhance their interests as they grow.

Staff

Our staff are experienced early years practitioners,friendly and welcoming.
